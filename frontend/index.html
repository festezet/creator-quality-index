Why this index?

YouTube's algorithm optimizes for one thing: ad revenue. It pushes watch time and engagement, not quality. A clickbait video with millions of views ranks higher than a meticulously researched documentary with a fraction of the audience. The platform rewards creators who game the system, not those who respect yours. This index exists to fix that — every channel here is scored by humans on research depth, production, signal-to-noise ratio, originality, and lasting impact. No algorithm. No sponsorship bias. Just substance.
